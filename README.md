## 💼 Severance Pay Calculator — Featured Project

**[severancecalculator.xyz](https://severancecalculator.xyz)**

The only free global severance pay calculator covering **8 countries** with official 2025 labor law formulas.

### Countries Covered

| Country | Law / Formula |
|---------|--------------|
| 🇺🇸 USA | 1 week per year (industry standard) |
| 🇬🇧 UK | Statutory Redundancy Pay — age-based, £643/week cap |
| 🇦🇪 UAE | 21 days/yr (first 5 yrs) + 30 days/yr after |
| 🇮🇳 India | (15 × salary × years) ÷ 26 — max ₹20 lakhs |
| 🇵🇰 Pakistan | 30 days wages per completed year |
| 🇵🇭 Philippines | ½ month per year (authorized causes) |
| 🇸🇦 Saudi Arabia | ½ month/yr (first 5) + 1 month/yr after |
| 🇨🇦 Canada | ~1 month per year (common law) |

### Features
- ✅ 8 countries, 2025 labor laws
- ✅ Handles resignation vs layoff vs fired
- ✅ Shows legal basis for every calculation
- ✅ 8 in-depth country guides / blog posts
- ✅ Full FAQ with Schema markup
- ✅ Google AdSense integrated
- ✅ Mobile-friendly, fast, no JS frameworks
- ✅ SEO optimized — meta tags, OG, structured data, sitemap
